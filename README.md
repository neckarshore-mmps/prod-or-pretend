# Prod or Pretend

**A constructive quality mirror for tech hype on LinkedIn and X.**

Prod or Pretend analyzes hyped tech posts against real-world production standards. Not a debunker — a mirror. When someone claims they "built this in a weekend," we ask: show us the repo. Then we look at what matters: tests, security, scalability, documentation. If there's no repo, we reflect the claim against a consultant's quality framework.

The goal is not to tear down, but to show what production-ready actually looks like — and to help decision-makers (CTOs, founders, team leads) distinguish substance from air.

## FAQ

**What is Prod or Pretend?**
A constructive quality mirror for hyped tech posts on LinkedIn and X. Not a debunker — a mirror. When someone says they "built this in a weekend," we ask to see the repo, then look at what actually matters: tests, security, scalability, documentation.

**Is it live yet?**
No. It's in the planning phase — the concept and the 12-point framework are defined, implementation hasn't started. This repo is the open documentation of that thinking.

**Are you here to tear people down?**
No. The goal is to show what production-ready actually looks like and help decision-makers tell substance from air — constructively, against a consultant's quality framework.

**How do you judge a claim?**
Against the 12-point Hype Checklist (see [docs/hype-checklist.md](docs/hype-checklist.md)): repo presence, tests, security, scalability, docs, and more.

**Is this a business?**
It's transparently an acquisition channel for Neckarshore AI consulting — not a revenue product. We say so out loud in [docs/monetization.md](docs/monetization.md).
