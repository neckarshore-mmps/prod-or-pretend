# Channel Strategy

How Prod or Pretend operates across platforms.

## Channels

| Channel | Brand | Tone | Automation | Human Loop | Status |
|---------|-------|------|-----------|------------|--------|
| LinkedIn | neckarshore.ai | Professional, constructive-critical | AI prepares, human finalizes | Always | MVP |
| X / Twitter | Prod or Pretend | Loose, direct, humorous | Fully automated (Phase 4) | Optional | MVP (manual) |
| Anonymous | TBD | Radical, provocative | TBD | TBD | Deferred (backlog) |

## LinkedIn Strategy

### Tone
- Professional, clean, constructive-critical
- Delivers results and next steps
- Makes life better for stakeholders in software development
- Original poster should not feel attacked
- Brand should not be damaged by any interaction

### Output Types
1. **Own posts** on neckarshore.ai timeline — primary format
2. **Reposts with commentary** — when original post has high reach
3. **Articles** — deep-dive analyses for high-value targets
4. **No direct commenting on original posts by default** — too risky, too intrusive

### Interaction Rules
1. Maintained watch-list of profiles (manual curation initially)
2. Max one interaction per person per week
3. Max 2-3 total interactions per person, then leave them alone
4. Only engage if first or second commenter — otherwise no added value
5. High-reach influencers: increase gap, reduce count (maybe just 1)
6. Goal: original poster does not realize what is behind the analysis

### Platform Rules
- No external links in LinkedIn posts (algorithm penalizes them)
- Platform-native content only
- Short lines, hook in first line, question or CTA at end

## X / Twitter Strategy

### Tone
- Loose, direct, humorous
- Constructively critical at core
- Lower barrier than LinkedIn
- No screaming, no trashing

### Approach (MVP)
- Manual posting, low frequency
- No creator setup, no monetization
- Cross-reference LinkedIn content where format fits
- Separate visual identity from LinkedIn (TBD)

## Channel Rollout

Sequential, not simultaneous:
1. LinkedIn first — build credibility, measure KPIs
2. X second — expand reach with looser format
3. Additional channels only if LinkedIn + X prove the format

## Content Principles

- **Mirror, don't debunk.** Reflect reality, show the professional path forward.
- **Solution-oriented.** Not "here are 5 problems with your code" but "here's what production-ready looks like."
- **No conflict, no war.** Don't wake the bear. Dosed, targeted, respectful.
- **Fact-based.** Every claim in an analysis must be verifiable. No opinions disguised as facts.
