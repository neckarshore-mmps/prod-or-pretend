# Monetization Strategy

Prod or Pretend is not a revenue product — it is an acquisition channel for Neckarshore AI consulting services.

## Business Model

**Primary:** Content builds reputation → reputation generates inbound leads → leads become consulting mandates under neckarshore.ai.

**Funnel:**
```
LinkedIn reach → Article views → Profile visits → Contact requests → Conversations → Mandate
```

## KPIs

### Awareness (non-monetary)

| # | KPI | Measurement | Frequency |
|---|-----|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Follower growth | LinkedIn + X follower count | Monthly |
| 2 | Post reach | Impressions per post | Per post |
| 3 | Engagement rate | Comments + shares (not just likes) | Per post |
| 4 | Article views | Views per publication | Per publication |

### Conversion (monetary)

| # | KPI | Measurement | Frequency |
|---|-----|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Profile visits | Post-publication profile views | Weekly |
| 2 | Contact requests | Inbound messages per month | Monthly |
| 3 | Calendly bookings | Who enters the sales funnel? | Monthly |
| 4 | Conversation requests | Who schedules a first call? | Monthly |
| 5 | Conversion rate | Conversation → mandate | Quarterly |

### Quality

| # | KPI | Measurement | Frequency |
|---|-----|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Detection accuracy | False positive rate | Ongoing (Phase 1) |
| 2 | Analysis quality | Engagement on analysis posts | Per post |
| 3 | Brand sentiment | Positive vs. "troll" perception | Quarterly |

> **Gap:** The connection between LinkedIn activity and actual sales often happens outside LinkedIn (email, phone, in-person). Must be manually tracked until CRM automates it.

## CRM Strategy

**Current state:** LinkedIn as primary contact manager, Calendly for booking, Outlook for email. Sufficient for MVP.

**Principle:** A CRM that isn't used is worse than no CRM. Implement only when contacts start slipping through cracks.

**Future options:**

| # | Tool | Pro | Con |
|---|------|-----|-----|
| 1 | HubSpot Free | LinkedIn integration, Calendly connection, email tracking | Expensive tiers fast |
| 2 | Pipedrive | Popular in DACH, sales-funnel focused, fair pricing | Less marketing automation |

**Decision:** Deferred until friction forces it.

## Ethical Guardrails

- No paywall on core analysis content (defeats credibility purpose)
- No sponsored analyses (destroys independence)
- No affiliate income from analyzed tools (conflict of interest)
- No fake engagement or bot amplification
